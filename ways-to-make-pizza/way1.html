Classic Oven-Baked Pizza🍕

Ingredients

  1. Tomato sauce (base)
  2. Mozzarella cheese
  3. Sliced black olives
  4. Sun-dried tomatoes
  5. 2Feta cheese crumbles
  6. Fresh basil leaves
  7. Drizzle of olive oil after baking

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°C (480°F).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce, add cheese, and toppings.
  4. Bake on a pizza stone or baking tray for 8–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melts.
