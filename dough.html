🍕How To Make A Perfect Pizza Dough🍕

Ingredients

  1. 2 ½ cups (300 g) all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tsp salt
  3. 1 tsp sugar
  4. 2 tsp dry yeast
  5. 2 tsp olive oil
  6. ¾ cup (180 ml) warm water

Step by steps Guide

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  3. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt.
  4. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  5. Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic
  6. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth.
  7. Let it rise for 1–2 hours in a warm place until it doubles in size.
